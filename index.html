Biryani

Make this classic Indian dish and ensure deliciously moist lamb with paneer, rice and all spiced to perfection.

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 50 mins
  • Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 tbsp finely grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 200g basmati rice, rinsed well
  • 400ml good-quality lamb or chicken stock
  • chopped coriander
